Mushroom gravy

mushroom recipe which taste so good with fried rice,noodles,chapathi,rice,etc as per your choices. Spicy chilli mushroom gravy which is so delicious.

Ingredients

  • Mushroom - 200 grams
  • Soy Sauce - 3 tsp
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • All Purpose Flour / Maida - Â¼ cup
  • Cornflour / Cornstarch - 2 tblsp
  • Onion - 1 chopped finely
  • Capsicum / Bell Pepper - 1 sliced thinly
  • Green Chilli - 2 chopped finely
  • Garlic - 4 cloves chopped
  • Vinegar - 1 tsp
  • Red Chilli Flakes - 2 tsp
  • Spring Onion as needed
  • Oil - 3 tblsp

Process

  1. Step 1Take mushrooms, salt, pepper, cornflour, all purpose flour, soy sauce in a bowl and mix well. Add little water if needed.
  2. Step 2Heat 2 tblsp of oil in a nonstick pan, add mushroom and saute till golden. Remove and set aside.
  3. Step 3Heat 1 tblsp oil in a pan. Add onions, chillies and garlic. Saute for a min.
  4. Step 4Add in capsicum and mix well.
  5. Step 5Add in soy sauce, sugar, salt, vinegar and mix well.
  6. Step 6Add in soy sauce, sugar, salt, vinegar and mix well.
  7. Step 7Add mushroom back in and bring it to a boil.
  8. Step 8Add the remaining cornflour mixture and mix well.
  9. Step 9Add in spring onions and mix well.
  10. Serve
