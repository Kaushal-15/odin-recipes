chicken biryani

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil,
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 medium tomatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh mint leaves
  • ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1 (2 inch) piece cinnamon stick
  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks
  • 1 pound basmati rice
  1. Step 1 Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2 Add 2 tablespoons of oil to the skillet. Add onions, garlic, and fresh ginger; cook and stir until onion is soft and golden. Add tomatoes, salt, cumin, chili powder, pepper, and turmeric; cook, stirring constantly, for 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3 Stir in yogurt, mint, ground cardamom, and cinnamon stick. Cover and cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes are cooked to a pulp. It may be necessary to add a little hot water if mixture becomes too dry and starts to stick to the pan.
  4. Step 4 Add chicken and stir well to coat. Cover and cook over very low heat until chicken is tender, 35 to 45 minutes. There should only be a little very thick gravy left when chicken is finished cooking. If necessary cook uncovered for a few minutes to reduce the gravy.
  5. Step 5 Meanwhile, make the rice: Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Step 6 Heat oil in a large skillet. Add onion; cook and stir until golden. Add cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, ground ginger, and saffron; stir in rice until coated with spices.
  7. Step 7 Heat stock and salt in a medium pot until hot; pour over rice and stir well.
  8. Step 8 Add chicken mixture; stir gently to combine. Bring to a boil.
  9. Step 9 Reduce heat to very low, cover with a tight-fitting lid, and steam for 20 minutes without lifting the lid or stirring,decor the biryani with coriander leaves and serve hot with curd and enjoy it.
