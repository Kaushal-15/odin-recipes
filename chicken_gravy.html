Chicken gravy

Chicken Gravy Recipe, A family favorite recipe made with onion, tomato base. Chicken is marinated with yogurt, spices, ginger & garlic. The chicken is cooked in onion, tomato and capsicum base which is thick and a layer of oil floating on top. This is a delicious chicken gravy recipe which can be served with naan and ghee rice.

Ingredients

For Marination

  • 1 kg Chicken bone-in cut into small pieces
  • ▢1 tbsp Chilli Powder
  • 2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ cup Yogurt / Curd
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste

    • For Tempering

  • 5 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 stick Cinnamon Stick

    • For Chicken Gravy Base

  • 4 large Onion sliced thinly
  • 1 Capsicum sliced thinly
  • 4 large Tomatoes sliced thinly
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1 tbsp Chilli Powder
  • 2 tbsp Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • 2 tbsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Green Chilli Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp Coriander leaves finely chopped

Instructions

  1. Step 1Mix chicken with the marination ingredients and set aside for 30 mins. Now take this in pressure cooker, add 1 cup of water and mix well.
  2. Step 2Cover and pressure cook for 2 whistle, lower the flame and cook for 10 mins. Now turn off the heat and let the steam go all by itself.
  3. Step 3Now heat oil in a kadai, add in fennel, cumin and cinnamon.
  4. Step 4Add in onions, salt and saute till lightly golden.
  5. Step 5Add in capsicum and tomatoes and cook covered till mushy.
  6. Step 6Now add in the spice powders and mix well.
  7. Step 7Add in ketchup, soy sauce and green chilli sauce and mix well.
  8. Step 8Add in the cooked chicken with any liquid and mix well.
  9. Step 9Now simmer this for 10 to 15 mins.
  10. Step 10Add in coriander leaves and mix well.
  11. Step 11serve
